Why We Exist
We build infrastructure so that thousands of organisations never have to think about infrastructure.

Twelve years ago, our founders were running platform engineering at companies that kept hitting the same wall: enterprise software that was either impossibly complex or dangerously fragile. They decided to build the alternative.

Today, NEXACORE's products process over 18 million API calls a day. The people who build them are given clear problems, ownership over solutions, and the expectation that they will raise issues the moment they find them.

What You Get

Built for People Who
Do Serious Work

These are the things that actually matter to engineers and operators who want to do their best work and maintain a life outside of it.

01
01 — Time
Async-First, No Meeting Days
Tuesdays and Thursdays are meeting-free by default across the whole organisation. Deep work is protected, not squeezed into calendar gaps. Flexible hours for all roles outside of core operational windows.
02
02 — Compensation
Top-Quartile Pay and Equity
Salaries are benchmarked at the 75th percentile of the market and reviewed annually. Equity refreshes are standard. We do not make lowball offers and negotiate up — we start at what the role is worth.
03
03 — Growth
Learning Budget With No Approval Gates
Every employee receives $3,000 annually for courses, conferences, books, or certifications. No approval process, no justification form — spend it on anything that makes you better at your work.
04
04 — Remote
Home Office Stipend and Coworking Access
$1,500 home office setup allowance on day one. If you prefer a coworking space, we cover up to $400/month at any WeWork or equivalent location worldwide. The right environment is yours to define.
05
05 — Health
Comprehensive Medical, Dental, Vision
Full medical, dental, and vision coverage with 100% employer-paid premium for employees and 80% for dependents. Mental health coverage and therapy sessions are included with no separate referral needed.
06
06 — Time Off
Unlimited PTO With a 15-Day Minimum
Unlimited paid time off with a required minimum of 15 days annually tracked by managers. Parental leave is 16 weeks fully paid regardless of gender. Additional sabbaticals at five-year tenure milestones.
Life at NEXACORE

The Culture Is the
Product, Too.

We do not have a culture slide deck. We have working practices that reflect what we actually value — transparency, craft, and the kind of boredom that comes from processes that run exactly as expected.

Engineering Culture
We Write Postmortems Before Someone Asks For Them
Incidents are learning opportunities. Every major issue results in a public internal postmortem within 72 hours, with root cause, timeline, and prevention steps.
Transparency
Monthly All-Hands. Company P&L Shared Internally.
Ownership
Engineers Ship to Production. No Separate Deployment Team.
Distributed Team
42 Nationalities. One Weekly Standup. Asynchronous Everything Else.
Time zones are respected through documentation standards and async tooling. No one attends a meeting that could have been a Loom.
Feedback
Quarterly 360 Reviews. Public Recognition.
